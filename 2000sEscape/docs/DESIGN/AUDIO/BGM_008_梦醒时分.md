# BGM-008: 梦醒时分

## 基本信息

| 属性 | 值 |
|------|-----|
| 编号 | BGM-008 |
| 名称 | 梦醒时分 |
| 用途 | 失败/死亡界面 |
| 目标时长 | 45-60秒 |
| 循环 | 否 (一次性播放) |
| 优先级 | P1 (次要) |

## 使用场景

**失败界面**
- 玩家在关卡中生命值归零
- 被敌人击杀
- 撤离失败

**场景描述**:
> 视线开始模糊，周围的声音逐渐远去。那些扭曲的黑影逼近，但你已无力反抗。记忆开始消散...这次，你没能带走任何东西。但梦还未结束，你还会再次醒来。

## 风格参考

### 参考风格
- **Dream Ambient**: 梦境氛围 - 消散中的幻梦
- **Y2K Nostalgia**: 千禧年怀旧 - 夏日午后醒来的感觉
- **Lo-fi Nature**: 低保真自然音 - 带着磁带质感的蝉鸣鸟叫

### 参考作品
- *Dark Souls* 的死亡界面 (但更温暖)
- *Omori* 的悲伤时刻
- *Yume Nikki* 醒来时的感觉
- 千禧年中国夏天午后的环境音

### 配器设想
```
核心:
- 柔和的合成器 Pad (极简，若有若无)
- 低沉的温暖 Drone
- 蝉鸣 (远处，有Lo-fi处理)
- 鸟叫 (稀疏，像是刚从梦中醒来听到的)

氛围:
- 夏日午后/黄昏的感觉
- 磁带/收音机的温暖质感
- "睁眼醒来"的朦胧感
- 梦境与现实交界处的模糊

环境音元素:
- 远处的蝉鸣 (经典的中国童年夏天声音)
- 偶尔的鸟叫 (稀疏，空洞)
- 可选: 远处的风吹树叶声
- 可选: 隐约的远处人声 (但听不清，像是另一个世界)

情绪:
- 从美好中被抽离出来的空虚
- 刚才还在梦里很开心，现在什么都没有了
- 恍惚、不真实感
- 像是小时候玩得太开心突然被叫回家的那种怅然若失
- 不是悲伤，是空洞
```

## 失败流程与音乐

```
玩家死亡
    │
    ↓ 硬切/渐暗
    │
┌───────────────────────┐
│  失败画面              │ ← 音乐开始，低沉
│  (模糊的视觉)          │
└───────────┬───────────┘
            │
            ↓
┌───────────────────────┐
│  失败信息              │ ← 音乐继续
│  "回忆消散..."         │
└───────────┬───────────┘
            │
            ↓
┌───────────────────────┐
│  重试选项              │ ← 音乐渐弱或保持
│  [再试一次]            │
└───────────┴───────────┘
```

## 设计要点

**不过度惩罚**:
- 游戏是关于"拯救自己"的，失败不应该太绝望
- 需要有"梦还未结束"的暗示
- 鼓励玩家再试一次

**音乐特征**:
- 开始: 突然的低落 (配合死亡)
- 中段: 安静的反思
- 结尾: 微妙的希望暗示 (为重试铺垫)

## SUNO Prompt

### 主Prompt (推荐 - 从快乐中抽离的空虚)

```
Style: Y2K emptiness, post-dream void, hollow ambient, dissociative atmosphere
Instruments: Empty synth pads, distant hollow cicadas, sparse bird calls that sound far away, cold drone underneath
Mood: Empty, hollow, dissociated, withdrawn, the void after joy ends, detached from reality
Tempo: Very slow or none, suspended in nothing
Structure: Static, going nowhere, stuck in emptiness
Production: Dry but distant, cold emptiness with warm undertones, lo-fi but hollow
Description: The feeling of being pulled out of a happy dream into nothing. You were just having such a good time, and now there's nothing. Not sad - empty. Like being a kid playing happily and suddenly being called home for dinner. The joy is gone but you don't know what to feel now. Just hollow. Distant cicadas and birds that sound like they're from another world. The emptiness after the party ends. Not depression, just... nothing.
```

### 备选Prompt 1 (更恍惚/不真实感)

```
Style: Dissociative ambient, unreal reality, dream withdrawal, gray afternoon
Instruments: Detached synth pads, muffled world sounds, cicadas that seem to echo in empty space, thin drones
Mood: Dissociated, unreal, floating in gray, not quite here, disconnected
Tempo: No pulse, suspended animation
Production: Muffled, distant, like hearing the world through water or glass
Description: The feeling when you wake up from a really good dream and reality feels fake. You're here but you're not. The cicadas outside sound distant, like they belong to a different world. Everything feels slightly wrong. You were so happy just now. Where did it go? Not sad, just... not anything. Hollow. The gray feeling after color fades.
```

### 备选Prompt 2 (更怅然若失感)

```
Style: Wistful emptiness, lost-in-transition ambient, post-joy void
Instruments: Sparse synth notes that go nowhere, distant summer sounds, hollow space between sounds
Mood: Lost, adrift, wistful emptiness, something just ended but nothing began
Tempo: Barely moving, stuck in between
Production: Lots of empty space, silence between sounds, dry and hollow
Description: The emptiness when happiness ends and nothing has replaced it yet. Like the moment after laughing with friends when everyone goes quiet. Like finishing a great game and staring at the menu screen. The cicadas are still there but they don't mean anything anymore. You were just somewhere better. Now you're here. Not sad - just empty. The space where joy used to be.
```

## 与重试的衔接

```
失败音乐结束
    │
    ├─→ 玩家选择重试 → 淡入关卡音乐
    │
    └─→ 玩家返回大厅 → 淡入梦境大厅音乐
```

**过渡要点**:
- 失败音乐结束后，无论选择什么都需要平滑过渡
- 可以用混响尾音连接

## 验收标准

### 必须满足
- [ ] 时长 45-60秒，不需要循环
- [ ] 空虚感，不是悲伤
- [ ] "从快乐中抽离"的感觉
- [ ] 安静、不刺耳

### 优先满足
- [ ] 有恍惚/不真实感
- [ ] 有蝉鸣和鸟叫的环境音 (但听起来遥远、空洞)
- [ ] 千禧年夏日的氛围
- [ ] 情绪是"空洞"而非"悲伤"

### 加分项
- [ ] 有"刚才还很好，现在什么都没有"的落差感
- [ ] 环境音与音乐融合自然
- [ ] 让玩家感到怅然若失而非被惩罚
