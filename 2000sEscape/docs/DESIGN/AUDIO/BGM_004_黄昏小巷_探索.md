| 属性 | 值 |

| 名称 | 黄昏小巷 - 探索 |

## 使用场景

**童年小巷 - 探索阶段**
- 玩家刚进入关卡，开始搜集回忆物品
- 敌人尚未发现玩家，或距离较远
- 可以自由探索，但也有潜在威胁

**场景描述**:
> 黄昏时分的老式居民区小巷。夕阳斜照，影子被拉得很长。熟悉的小卖部、自行车棚、电线杆...一切都那么眼熟，却又哪里不对劲。远处偶尔传来模糊的人声，但转过街角，空无一人。这里安静得有些不自然。

## 风格参考

### 参考风格
- **Dream Ambient**: 千禧年梦幻氛围 - 早期数字合成器的飘渺感
- **Y2K Dreamscape**: 千禧年幻梦 - 90年代末2000年代初的梦境美学
- **Glitch Vaporwave (轻量)**: 轻度故障蒸汽波 - 温暖的损坏感
- **Space Music**: 太空音乐 - 广阔、空灵、悬浮感

### 参考作品
- *Yume Nikki* 的世界探索 (那种飘渺的梦境感)
- *Silent Hill* 的迷雾街道 (但不那么恐怖)
- *PS1菜单界面* 的等待音乐
- *Early 2000s New Age* 的氛围音乐
- *Dream House* 去掉节拍的氛围版

### 配器设想
```
核心:
- 柔和的早期数字合成器 Pad (不要钢琴、不要中国乐器)
- 极简的、若有若无的音色
- 飘渺的、像在云端的质感

氛围:
- 低频的温暖 Drone (不是紧张，是包裹感)
- 磁带的轻微抖动
- 黄昏的"金色"温暖质感
- 早期数字混响的"假"空间感

千禧年元素:
- 早期CD/MD时代的"干净但有瑕疵"的音质
- 像是2000年初电梯/商场里那种梦幻背景音
- 偶尔的数字故障感 (但很轻微)
- 环境采样只作为遥远背景 (不是乐器)

注意:
- 不要任何明显的旋律乐器 (钢琴/二胡都太吵)
- 不要任何"民族"乐器
- 音乐应该是"不存在"的背景感
- 让环境音成为主角，音乐只是底色
```

## SUNO Prompt

### 主Prompt (推荐 - 千禧年幻梦)

```
Style: Y2K dream ambient, early digital dreamscape, soft vaporwave, space music, floating atmosphere
Instruments: Warm early digital synth pads only, no piano, no acoustic instruments, subtle tape texture, gentle low drone
Mood: Dreamy, floating, nostalgic haze, beautiful emptiness, liminal warmth, golden hour daydream
Tempo: 60-70 BPM or free-form, extremely slow and spacious
Structure: Barely there, wallpaper music, looping atmosphere with minimal movement
Production: Early 2000s digital warmth, CD-era clarity with subtle imperfections, wide reverb, soft high frequencies
Description: Exploration music that barely exists. Like the background music in a dream you can almost remember. Early 2000s elevator music but beautiful and haunting. Floating synth pads with no clear melody. The feeling of walking through an empty mall at dusk in 2002. Not sad, not happy, just suspended in golden light. No piano, no ethnic instruments - just soft digital warmth.
```

### 备选Prompt 1 (更飘渺空灵)

```
Style: Space ambient, ethereal drone, new age atmosphere, zero gravity dreams
Instruments: Extremely soft synth pads, filtered noise floor, minimal harmonic movement, breath-like textures
Mood: Floating, weightless, timeless, suspended, peaceful void
Tempo: No discernible tempo, free-floating
Production: Clean but warm, early digital reverb, subtle noise, very wide stereo
Description: Music that sounds like floating in a golden void. Almost nothing happens - just warm chords changing very slowly. Like the menu screen of a PS1 game that you left on while falling asleep. The most minimal possible music that still feels like something. Peaceful but with a hint of something unspoken.
```

### 备选Prompt 2 (更千禧年商场/电梯感)

```
Style: Y2K mall music, early 2000s elevator ambient, dreamy background, soft Muzak aesthetic
Instruments: Warm digital synthesizers, soft string pad simulation, gentle ambient wash
Mood: Nostalgic suspension, pleasant waiting, beautiful limbo, shopping mall at closing time
Tempo: 65 BPM, barely moving
Production: Early 2000s smooth production, gentle compression, warm EQ, polite reverb
Description: The feeling of being in a nearly empty shopping mall in 2001 as the sun sets through the skylights. Soft synth music playing from distant speakers. Beautiful but slightly melancholic. Like waiting for something that might never come. Elevator music that's actually good. No drums, no piano, no singing - just floating chords.
```
